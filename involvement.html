Volunteering

Stark Library - Jackson Community Branch

Helper (2023) — ~70 hours

I volunteered regularly at the Jackson Community Branch, where I organized and reshelved books, prepared materials for community events, and created an inviting space for younger readers. I often helped children choose books suited to their interests, encouraged reading, and assisted visitors in locating items throughout the library.

VBS

Drama Lead & Games Head (2023–2025) — ~35 hours

At VBS, I served as both the lead actor in the children’s drama and the head of games for students aged 4–14. I designed and set up each day’s games, taught the rules, and organized smooth rotations between stations such as crafts, lessons, and activities.

Home and Garden Show

Event Volunteer (2023) — ~3 hours

I managed ticket sales and admissions for the Home and Garden Show at the Hall of Fame, accurately processing payments and tracking attendance for hundreds of visitors.

Haunted Hayride
